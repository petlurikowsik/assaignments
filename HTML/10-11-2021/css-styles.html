Preface iv

About the Book

This is designed as a book textbook to cater to the requirement of the first course ib python programming. It is suited for undergraduate degree students of computer science eingineering and information technology as well as postgraduate students of computer application. The objective of this book is to introduce the students to the fundamentals of computers and the concepts of python programming language, and enable them to apply these concepts for solving real-world problems.

Key Special features of the book

The following are the key features of the book:

  • Offers simple and lucid treatment of concepts supported with illustration for easy understanding.
  • Contains separate chapters on Strings, Files, Excepion Handling, and Operator Overloading.
  • Point-wise summary and glossary of keyterms to aid quick recapitulation to concepts.
  • Provides numerous programming examples along with their outputs to help students master the art of writing efficient python programs.
