HISTORY OF COMPUTERS

History of computers can be understood by looking into five generations. With each new generation of computers, there had been advancement in computer technology.

  1. First generation(1942-1955)

    2. Hardware technology First generation computers were manufactured using thousands of vacuum tubes. Vacuum is a device made of fragile glass.

  2. Second generation(1955-1964)

    3. Hardware technology second generation computers were manufactured using transistors. Transistors were reliable, powerful, cheaper, smaller, and cooler than vacuum tubes.

  3. Third generation(1964-1975)

    4. Hardware technology Third generation computers were manufactured using integrated chips. ICs consists of several components such as transistors, capacitors, and resistors on a single chip to avoid wired interconnection between components.
